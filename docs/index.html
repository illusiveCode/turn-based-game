Game Rules

  • Move a maximum of 3 spaces horizontally or vertically.
  • Pick up weapons to increase damage stats.
  • Move to an adjacent square to the opponent to begin fight sequence.
  • Choose to attack, defend or run.
  • Who ever has their health drop to 0 will lose the game.

    • Good Luck!

MAKE YOUR MOVE!

Attack: You will deal full damage to enemy and recieve full damage.

Defend: You will experience 50% less damage from the enemy.

Run: Take 50% less damage and make a move to escape.
